TPCGEMSimulation (v1.10)

*This simulation package is dedicated to detailed studies of a Time *Projection Chamber (TPC) with a Gas Electron Multiplier (GEM) readout.

Overview

*It consists of for Modules
*1. Primary ionisation
*2. Drifting the electrons including diffusion
*3. Amplification in a triple GEM structure
*4. Shaping and binning of charge in ADC counts
